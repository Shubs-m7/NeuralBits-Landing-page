About Us

NeuralBits Technologies Pvt Ltd, founded in 2013 in Mumbai, specializes in affordable, homegrown IT solutions for the healthcare sector. Over the past decade, the company has implemented advanced cloud-based healthcare software for hundreds of hospitals, nursing homes, and clinics across India.

Unlike traditional client-server systems, NeuralBits offers a secure, scalable, and fully integrated healthcare management suite on the cloud, enabling more efficient and accessible healthcare services.
